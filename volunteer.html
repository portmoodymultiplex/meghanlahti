Volunteer.

A city shaped by the people who show up. Be one of them.

Why Your Help Matters

Every door. Every call. Every sign.

Meghan built her 2022 victory on a strong volunteer base and a clear message. In 2026, the stakes are higher and the competition is real. The campaign needs people who believe in Port Moody’s future to show up — in every neighbourhood, at every door.

You don’t need experience. You need a couple of hours and a belief that this city deserves steady, experienced leadership.

Door Knocking

The most effective way to reach voters. We’ll pair you with a partner, give you a walk list, and train you on the script. Shifts run June through October.

Phone Banking

Call supporters, remind voters to advance vote, check in on pledges. Can be done from home. Especially valuable in the final two weeks before election day.

Sign Placement

Help install and maintain lawn signs across key neighbourhoods. Starts at the official campaign launch in June.

Events & Community

Help at town halls, community events, and the campaign launch. If you enjoy connecting with people, this is for you.

Ready to get involved?

Fill out our form and the campaign team will be in touch within 48 hours.
